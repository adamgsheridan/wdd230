Sugar Hill City Hall

Our city hall stands for all of our friends, families, and visitors to get together and shape the future of Sugar Hill. It's fine architecture and well-maintained grounds allow for exciting events and lasting memories.

The Bowl

The Bowl is an Amphitheater located at the town center here at Sugar Hill. Its grassy terraces allow poeple to comfortably sit or stand as they enjoy performances or other kinds of events.

The Eagle Theatre

Come see live performances of some of the best music in town. The Eagle Theatre is the perfect venue for a night in town.

City Hall Gym

Here at the Sugar Hill City Hall we have a gym open for the public. It's complete with a basketball court, a weightroom, and much more.

Splash Pad

There's no better way to beat the heat than spend the day at the Sugar Hill Splash Pad. When you're looking to beat the heat on a summer afternoon, it's a no-brainer!

Veterans Memorial

Out of respect for our troops we have a beautiful memorial for the heroes who have sacrificed everything to give us the freedoms that we have today.
