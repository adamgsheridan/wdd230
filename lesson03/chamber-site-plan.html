What kinds of things is Rexburg known for?

Rexburg is known for being the home of Brigham Young University Idaho, a religious university associated with the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It is also the center of a lot of cultural events.

Who founded Rexburg, Idaho

Thomas Ricks founded Rexburg, Idaho in 1883.

How did Rexburg get its's name?

It was named after a Navy electronics laboratory oceanographic research ship called the USS Rexburg.
